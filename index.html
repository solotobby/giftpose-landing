Features

Everything you need to gift effortlessly

Giftpose simplifies the entire gifting journey — from listing to delivery — in just a few taps.

🎁

Instant Listings

Snap a photo, add a brief description, and your item is live in under 30 seconds. No fluff, no friction.

💝

Smart Matching

Our algorithm connects the right giver with the right recipient based on location, interests, and wishlist data.

🔒

Safe & Verified

Every user is identity-verified. Every exchange is tracked. Give and receive with complete peace of mind.

📱

Mobile-First

Built for the way you actually live. Manage gifting on the go with our intuitive iOS and Android apps.

🌍

Community Impact

Every gift keeps an item out of landfill. Track your personal environmental impact right in the app.

Lightning Fast

Gifting shouldn't be complicated. We've distilled it to its purest form.

01

List Your Item

Upload a photo, write a short description, and choose who you'd like to give it to — everyone or a specific group.

02

Get Matched
